# Media Channel Specifications

## Television

### Broadcast TV
| Daypart | Hours | Index | CPM Range |
|---------|-------|-------|-----------|
| Early Morning | 6-9am | 80 | $8-12 |
| Daytime | 9am-4pm | 60 | $5-10 |
| Early Fringe | 4-7pm | 90 | $10-18 |
| Prime | 8-11pm | 150 | $25-50 |
| Late Fringe | 11pm-1am | 70 | $8-15 |
| Late Night | 1-6am | 40 | $3-8 |

### Connected TV (CTV/OTT)
- **Targeting**: Household, behavioral, contextual
- **Formats**: :15, :30, :60 pre-roll, mid-roll
- **Measurement**: Completion rates, reach, frequency
- **CPM Range**: $20-40

## Digital Video

### YouTube
- TrueView In-Stream: Skippable after 5s
- TrueView Discovery: Thumbnail placement
- Bumper Ads: 6-second non-skip
- Masthead: Homepage takeover

### Online Video (OLV)
- Pre-roll, mid-roll, outstream
- CPM Range: $10-25
- Target VCR: 70%+

## Paid Social

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
| Format | Specs | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Feed Image | 1080x1080 | Engagement |
| Feed Video | 1:1 or 4:5 | Awareness |
| Stories | 1080x1920 | Reach |
| Reels | 9:16 | Engagement |
| Carousel | Up to 10 cards | Traffic |

### TikTok
| Format | Duration | Best For |
|--------|----------|----------|
| In-Feed | 5-60s | Engagement |
| TopView | Up to 60s | Awareness |
| Spark Ads | Organic boost | Authenticity |

## Paid Search

### Google Ads
- Search campaigns: Text ads on SERP
- Performance Max: Cross-channel automation
- Local campaigns: Store-focused
- Discovery: YouTube, Gmail, Discover

### Microsoft Ads
- Similar to Google with LinkedIn integration
- Often 20-30% lower CPCs

## Display/Programmatic

### Standard IAB Sizes
- 300x250 Medium Rectangle
- 728x90 Leaderboard
- 160x600 Wide Skyscraper
- 300x600 Half Page
- 320x50 Mobile Leaderboard

### Buying Approaches
- Open Exchange: Broadest reach
- Private Marketplace (PMP): Premium inventory
- Programmatic Guaranteed: Reserved + automated

## Out-of-Home (OOH)

### Traditional
| Format | Reach | Frequency |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Bulletins | High | Low |
| Posters | Medium | Medium |
| Transit | Medium | High |
| Street Furniture | Low | High |

### Digital OOH (DOOH)
- Dynamic creative capability
- Daypart rotation
- Weather/trigger-based
- Programmatic buying available

## Audio

### Streaming Audio
- Spotify, Pandora, iHeart, Amazon Music
- :15, :30, :60 formats
- Companion display banners
- Podcast host-read and produced ads

### Terrestrial Radio
- AM/FM coverage
- Format targeting
- Daypart buying
- Live read endorsements
