# Agency Relationship Management Guide

## Agency Ecosystem Structure

### Media Agency of Record (AOR)
Primary agency responsible for media strategy, planning, and buying across all channels.

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Annual media planning and strategy
- Media buying and negotiations
- Campaign trafficking and launch
- Performance reporting and optimization
- Competitive intelligence
- Innovation and test recommendations

**Fee Structures:**
| Model | Description | Typical Range |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| Commission | % of media spend | 2-5% |
| Retainer | Fixed monthly fee | $15-50K/month |
| Hybrid | Base retainer + commission | Varies |
| Performance | Tied to KPIs | Variable |

### Specialized Partners

**Programmatic/DSP Partner**
- Platform management (DV360, TTD, Amazon)
- Audience strategy and data
- Brand safety implementation

**Social Agency**
- Platform expertise (Meta, TikTok, etc.)
- Community management
- Influencer relationships

**Search Agency**
- SEM management
- SEO coordination
- Local/Maps optimization

**Measurement Partner**
- MMM/Attribution modeling
- Brand lift studies
- Cross-channel measurement

## Performance Management Framework

### Quarterly Business Review (QBR) Structure

**1. Performance Review (30 min)**
- Channel-by-channel results vs. goals
- Key wins and challenges
- Competitive landscape update

**2. Strategic Discussion (20 min)**
- Upcoming quarter priorities
- Test and learn opportunities
- Resource needs

**3. Operational Review (10 min)**
- Process improvements
- Team updates
- Contract/SOW status

### Scorecard Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | Metrics |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Strategic Value | 25% | Quality of recommendations, proactivity |
| Execution Quality | 25% | Accuracy, timeliness, optimization |
| Communication | 20% | Responsiveness, clarity, partnership |
| Innovation | 15% | New ideas, test proposals, trends |
| Value/Efficiency | 15% | Cost efficiency, added value |

### Performance Ratings

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 9-10 | Exceptional | Recognition, expanded scope |
| 7-8.9 | Strong | Continue, minor improvements |
| 5-6.9 | Satisfactory | Improvement plan needed |
| 3-4.9 | Needs Improvement | Formal warning, intervention |
| <3 | Unacceptable | Review engagement |

## SOW Best Practices

### Essential SOW Components

1. **Scope of Services**
   - Specific deliverables
   - Channels covered
   - Geographic scope
   - Reporting requirements

2. **Team and Resources**
   - Named team members
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Minimum time commitment
   - Backup/coverage plans

3. **Fees and Terms**
   - Fee structure and rates
   - Payment terms
   - Out-of-scope charges
   - Rate lock period

4. **Performance Expectations**
   - KPIs and targets
   - Reporting cadence
   - Response time SLAs
   - Review periods

5. **Term and Termination**
   - Contract duration
   - Renewal terms
   - Termination notice
   - Transition obligations

### Annual Planning Cycle

| Month | Activity |
|-------|----------|
| September | Issue RFI/RFP if reviewing |
| October | Agency presentations, evaluation |
| November | SOW negotiation |
| December | Contract finalization |
| January | Annual planning kickoff |
| February | Media plan finalization |
| Ongoing | Quarterly reviews |

## Communication Protocols

### Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Status | Weekly | Working team | Tactical updates |
| Strategy | Bi-weekly | Leads | Strategic alignment |
| QBR | Quarterly | Leadership | Performance review |
| Annual Planning | Yearly | Full teams | Next year planning |

### Escalation Path

1. **Day-to-Day Issues** → Account Manager
2. **Tactical Concerns** → Account Director
3. **Strategic Issues** → Agency Leadership
4. **Contract/Relationship** → Procurement/Legal

### Communication Standards

- **Email Response**: Within 4 business hours
- **Urgent Issues**: Same-day resolution or escalation
- **Weekly Reports**: Delivered by Friday 5pm
- **Campaign Launches**: 48-hour advance confirmation

## Vendor Management Best Practices

### Onboarding New Partners

1. **Discovery Phase**
   - Business context briefing
   - Access and system setup
   - Historical data transfer
   - Team introductions

2. **Alignment Phase**
   - Strategy review
   - Process documentation
   - Reporting templates
   - Communication protocols

3. **Launch Phase**
   - Pilot campaign
   - Performance baseline
   - Feedback loop establishment

### Offboarding Partners

1. **Transition Planning**
   - Data export requirements
   - Knowledge transfer sessions
   - Platform access transition
   - Pending work handoff

2. **Documentation**
   - Campaign archives
   - Performance history
   - Learnings documentation
   - Contact handover

## Risk Management

### Common Risks and Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Key person departure | Cross-trained teams, documentation |
| Billing disputes | Clear SOW, monthly reconciliation |
| Underperformance | Early warning KPIs, escalation path |
| Conflict of interest | Disclosure requirements, category separation |
| Data security | Compliance audits, access controls |

### Contract Protections

- **Audit rights**: Right to review billings and processes
- **IP ownership**: Clear ownership of developed assets
- **Indemnification**: Protection against third-party claims
- **Insurance requirements**: E&O, cyber, general liability
