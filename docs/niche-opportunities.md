# Niche Opportunities: Profitable Use-Case Discovery

**Date:** 2025-12-13
**Purpose:** Identify and prioritize high-value niches for Skill Engine expansion

---

## Methodology

Niches evaluated against:
1. **Market Size:** Number of potential users and organizations
2. **Pain Intensity:** How severe and frequent the problems are
3. **AI Fit:** How well AI can address the core workflows
4. **Willingness to Pay:** Budget availability and ROI clarity
5. **Competitive Gap:** Lack of good existing solutions
6. **Platform Fit:** Alignment with existing Skill Engine capabilities

---

## 15 Potential Niches Evaluated

### Niche 1: AI Governance & Data Security
**Function:** Security, Risk, Compliance, IT Leadership

**Core Recurring Workflows:**
- AI usage policy development and updates
- Data flow mapping for AI systems
- Vendor AI security assessments
- AI incident response planning
- Regulatory compliance documentation (EU AI Act, state privacy laws)

**Pain Points:**
- Organizations adopting AI without governance frameworks
- Legal/compliance teams overwhelmed by AI-related questions
- Security teams lack AI-specific risk assessment tools
- No standardized templates for AI policies

**Why AI Can Help:**
- Generate comprehensive policy frameworks from requirements
- Map data flows and identify risk points systematically
- Create consistent assessment criteria across vendors
- Produce stakeholder-appropriate communications about AI risks/benefits

**Assessment:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest Priority)
- Exploding demand as AI adoption accelerates
- No dominant solution exists
- High willingness-to-pay (risk mitigation)
- Perfect platform fit
