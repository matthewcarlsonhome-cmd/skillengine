# Employee Onboarding Planner

## Metadata
- **ID**: employee-onboarding-planner
- **Category**: HR/People Operations
- **Time Saved**: 4-6 hours per new hire
- **Recommended Model**: Any

## Description
Create comprehensive onboarding plans with schedules, milestones, resources, and 30-60-90 day goals.

This skill generates thorough onboarding plans that accelerate new hire productivity and integration. It creates detailed schedules, stakeholder meetings, learning resources, success milestones, and customized 30-60-90 day plans.

## What You Get
- 30-60-90 Day Plan
- Week 1 Schedule
- Stakeholder Meetings
- Learning Resources
- Success Milestones
- Manager Checklist

## Inputs
| Field | Type | Required | Description |
|-------|------|----------|-------------|
| newHireName | text | Yes | New Hire Name & Role (e.g., John Doe, Senior Engineer) |
| startDate | text | Yes | Start Date (e.g., January 15, 2025) |
| teamContext | textarea | Yes | Team & Department - Describe the team, key projects, culture... |
| roleExpectations | textarea | Yes | Role Expectations - What should this person accomplish in their first 90 days? |
| keyStakeholders | textarea | Yes | Key Stakeholders - List people they should meet (name, role, why)... |
| tools | textarea | No | Tools & Systems - Software, systems, and tools they need access to... |

**ONBOARDING PHILOSOPHY:**
1. **Prepare Before Day 1**: Onboarding starts before they arrive
2. **Connections First**: Relationships matter more than paperwork
3. **Clarity**: Clear expectations from day one
4. **Quick Wins**: Early success builds confidence
5. **Feedback Loops**: Check in frequently early on
6. **Cultural Immersion**: Values and norms, not just tasks
7. **Personalized**: Tailor to role and individual
