## Job Seeker Skills (16)

Skills designed to accelerate your job search, from resume optimization to salary negotiation.

### 1. Job Readiness Score
**File:** `prompts/by-role/job-seeker/job-readiness-score.md`

**What it does:** Provides a quantified 0-100 assessment of how ready you are for a specific role. Analyzes your resume against the job description and identifies exactly where you stand.

**You get:**
- Overall readiness score with breakdown by category
- Skills match percentage with gap analysis
- Experience alignment assessment
- Education/certification fit evaluation
- Specific recommendations to improve your score

**Best for:** Before applying to understand your fit, or to prioritize which jobs to pursue.
